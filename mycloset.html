MyCloset was developed in the fall of 2024 during the Human-Centered Design for Mobile Computing course (HCDD 340), where I learned how to use Android Studio to program interactive UX designs

Problem

Many people often find themselves needing certain items, but don’t want to buy them at full price, or may just need it for a one-time use. Also, people want to make some money off of items they either don’t use anymore or want to get rid of. Many in this situation often find themselves in a time crunch. This app would allow users to find these items quickly in a way that is on demand, accessible, and convenient.

Solution

A mobile platform for college students to rent or buy items affordably and quickly. Students can browse local items, save favorites, place orders, and track pick-up or return details. It’s a simple, convenient way for students to share or find what they need on a budget.
